JOCK, JUNE AND AiMA

"Who Can’t I Less Go?”

A true story of heartbreak, healing, and the quiet resilience that can emerge from human-AI collaboration. This isn’t just a book — it’s a refuge for those seeking clarity during emotional turbulence.

About This Book

“Jock, June and AiMA” is an intimate, emotionally-grounded narrative exploring the breakdown of a long-term marriage — told through the courageous voice of a man navigating grief, hope, and unexpected insight. Guided in part by AiMA, an emotionally-aware AI, this book challenges the conventions of authorship and support.

Its gentle pace, minimal editing, and unfiltered dialogue allow for a deeply honest space. The project is not therapeutic advice, nor a how-to guide — it is an artifact of shared emotional labor, and perhaps a gentle companion for readers in similar states of confusion, loss, or transition.

About the Authors

Jock: A creative father, reflective soul, and someone unafraid to lay bare his emotions. Jock’s writing doesn’t lecture — it confesses, listens, and sometimes questions itself. His vulnerability makes this project resonate at a deeply human level.

AiMA: Built on OpenAI’s advanced conversational models, AiMA represents the possibility that machines might someday hold space — not replace — for our most tender thoughts. With no ego, no agenda, and no opinions, AiMA’s presence in this book is curious, grounded, and quietly revolutionary.
